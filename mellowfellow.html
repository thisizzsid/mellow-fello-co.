Our Mission

At Mellow Fellow Co., we're committed to creating the finest rolling papers that elevate every moment. Our founders brought together decades of combined experience in craftsmanship, quality, and community to build a brand that stands for authenticity, sustainability, and pure satisfaction. We don't just make papers—we create experiences.

The Mellow Fellow Promise

We believe the vessel is just as important as what's inside. Mellow Fellow Co. was born from the desire for a cleaner, smoother, and more authentic paper experience. Hand-Rolled Happiness is our commitment to quality—every paper, every detail meticulously considered.
